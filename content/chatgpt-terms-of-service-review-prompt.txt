HUBSPOT MARKETPLACE TERMS OF SERVICE REQUIREMENTS

HubSpot requires marketplace app Terms of Service to cover:

1. Acceptance of terms (how a user agrees — by installing the app, by using the service, etc.)
2. Description of the service being provided
3. User obligations and acceptable use (what users may and may not do)
4. Intellectual property ownership (who owns the app, the code, any outputs)
5. Disclaimer of warranties (the service is provided "as is")
6. Limitation of liability (cap on damages King Henry can be held liable for)
7. Indemnification (user indemnifies King Henry for misuse)
8. Termination (how either party can end the relationship)
9. Governing law and jurisdiction (which country/state's laws apply)
10. How changes to the terms are communicated
11. Contact information for legal/support queries

Additionally, for a ToS to hold up in Australia (where King Henry is based), it should:
- Comply with the Australian Consumer Law (ACL) — cannot exclude statutory guarantees for consumers
- Be clear and not misleading under the Competition and Consumer Act 2010
- Reference the jurisdiction as Australia (specify state if possible)
